Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to KeyPear. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website, use our Digital Wallet, or participate in the Pilot Program with the Hong Kong Blockchain Society (HKBCS).

2. The Data We Collect About You

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of organizational and personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data includes first name, last name, organizational role, and corporate identity (CorpID).
  • Contact Data includes email address, telephone numbers, and organizational addresses.
  • Technical Data includes internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting, and operating system.
  • Credential Data includes the verifiable cryptographic proofs issued to or held by your digital wallet. (Note: KeyPear does not store the content of your verifiable credentials on centralized servers; they are held entirely locally on your device).

3. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To process your application for the Pilot Program.
  • To manage our relationship with you, including notifying you about changes to our terms or privacy policy.
  • To administer and protect our business, platform, and digital wallet ecosystem.

4. Data Security & Cryptography

KeyPear utilizes advanced cryptography, decentralized identifiers (DIDs), and verifiable credentials strictly governed by W3C standards. Your private keys and the contents of your credentials remain securely on your local device. We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your basic account data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way.
