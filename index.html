Practical Use Cases in Hong Kong

See how SMEs and NGOs are leveraging verifiable credentials and local digital infrastructure to streamline operations and build instant trust.

B2B Vendor Onboarding

Instantly prove your Business Registration, compliance status, and corporate identity when onboarding as a supplier, eliminating weeks of manual due diligence.

Grant & Funding Applications

NGOs and eligible SMEs can securely present verifiable proof of their tax-exempt status, organizational structure, and operational history to government bodies and funding institutions.

Employee Representation

Issue verifiable delegate credentials to your staff. Empower representatives, inspectors, or agents to cryptographically prove they are authorized to act on behalf of your organization.

Professional Accreditations

Hold and present verified memberships, industry certifications (e.g., ISO standards), and HKBCS association credentials to unlock gated industry resources and build client trust instantly.

Form Pre-filling

Streamline government and corporate form submissions by automatically pre-filling verified organizational data—Business Registration number, company name, and director details—directly from your digital credentials.

Data Interchange

Exchange verified organizational data securely between institutions, regulators, and business partners using standardized credential formats—eliminating the need for repetitive document submissions and manual re-verification across counterparties.
